Welcome to Mudaris

Mudaris is a location based tutor finder. Which will reduce the gap between the learner and the teachers. Where students will be able to learn any skill they want in their vicinity and the skilled people (especially women) will get jobs at their home. Students / learner will be able to find out tutor / teacher nearer to them.

Why choose us?

We will connect you to students who are nearer to your living place.All you have to do is register on our system and pay very low amount from the earning you earn via MUDARIS.

We have got you 3 different teaching pacakages.Make use of your skills and thats too sitting in your home because we have students for you.

What we do?

We bridge the gap between teachers and students. Make student able to find a teacher by phone location. No charges for students. Just a very low % of fee amount for teacher.
